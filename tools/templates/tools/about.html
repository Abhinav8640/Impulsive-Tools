The short version

I kept needing small, boring things done — merge a couple of PDFs, throw together a QR code, work out a CGPA before results day — and every site that did it buried the actual tool behind three ad banners and a sign-up wall. So I started building my own, one tool at a time, and {{ site_name }} is where they all live now.

How it's organized

Everything sits in one of six workspaces — PDF, Student, Social Media, Security, Resume, and QR & Barcode. No search-and-hope; pick a workspace and everything in it is relevant to what you're doing.

What's actually live

Most tools here run for real — merging PDFs, generating QR codes, scoring a resume, all of it. A few of the heavier ones (converting PDFs to Word or PowerPoint, OCR) are marked "Coming Soon" because they need a proper conversion engine behind them, not because I forgot about them. They're on the list.
